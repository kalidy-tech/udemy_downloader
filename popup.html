How to Use

• Login to Udemy Account

You must have a Udemy account and connect it with this extension.

• Turn On Developer Mode

Go to More Tools → Extension → Turn on Developer Mode

• Save As Screen

If you disable the "Ask where to save before downloading" feature in Chrome settings, you can download videos in an organized manner.
Change this setting at: chrome://settings/downloads

• Download Folder Structure

Videos will be saved in the following structure:
Your Download Folder \ Udemy Downloads \ Instructor Name \ Course Name \ Video Name.mp4
Example: C:\Users\PC1\Downloads\Udemy Downloads\JosePortilla\CompletePython\1.CourseIntroduction.mp4

About Us


Udemy Downloader is an incredibly useful browser extension specifically designed to provide users with a seamless experience in downloading educational videos from Udemy. By simply clicking a button, users can effortlessly download their desired course videos directly to their local storage. This tool is perfect for students and lifelong learners who prefer to have offline access to their study materials. Whether you have a spotty internet connection or prefer to study without interruptions, this extension ensures that you have your Udemy videos at your fingertips, anytime and anywhere. ^^
